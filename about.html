About Dave

Dave is a highly successful scientist and engineer with over 35 years of experience developing food and beverage products for a Fortune 100 company. He has a Bachelor of Science from Truman State, a Master of Food Science from the University of Illinois and an MBA from DePaul University. Dave also has experience developing RTE Cereals, hot cereals, frozen foods, syrups, mixes, pet foods, beverages, and snack products.
